Uncle Nigel's Fried Rice Recipe

Fried Rice

This recipe is from Uncle Nigel who is an online personality that is well-known for his critques about fried rice from other cooks.

Ingredients

No quantity listed, just use feeling
  • Garlic
  • Shallot
  • Egg
  • Spring onion
  • Chilli
  • Chicken rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Asian seasame oil
  • Peanut online
  • MSG

Steps

  1. Oil the wok with oil
  2. Fry garlic and shallot until fragrant
  3. Add egg, fry until fluffy
  4. Add rice and stir
  5. Add soy sauce, sesame oil, and MSG
  6. Add spring onion and chilli
  7. Toss until done
